Mini Meal

The Mini Meal at Sahyadri Food Court is a delightful and satisfying option for those seeking a wholesome yet light meal. It features a serving of fluffy rice accompanied by a tangy and aromatic rasam, a flavorful side dish that varies daily, and a traditional pickle to add a zesty kick. Perfectly balanced and thoughtfully prepared, the Mini Meal offers a taste of home-cooked comfort, making it a favorite choice for many on campus.

Whether you're in between classes or looking for a quick lunch, the Mini Meal provides a nutritious and delicious break from your busy day. It's the ideal choice for a fulfilling meal that doesn't weigh you down, ensuring you stay energized and focused.

Special Meal

The Special Meal at Sahyadri Food Court is a feast fit for any occasion. This hearty meal includes a generous serving of rice, tangy rasam, two flavorful side dishes, two fluffy puris, a refreshing bowl of curd, and a traditional pickle to round out the flavors. Perfect for those times when you need a little extra nourishment, the Special Meal offers a complete and satisfying dining experience.

Ideal for a hearty lunch or dinner, this meal provides a balanced mix of textures and flavors that cater to all your cravings. With each component thoughtfully prepared, the Special Meal ensures you enjoy a delicious and wholesome dining experience every time.

Frequently asked questions

What are the login hours?

Our food court operates from 11:00 AM to 10:00 PM every day, ensuring our guests can enjoy our diverse menu throughout the day. Whether you're joining us for breakfast, snacks, or anything in between, we look forward to welcoming you during our regular hours of operation.

What do i get my refund?

If you're inquiring about a refund from a restaurant, you'll need to contact the food court manager directly. Reach out to their customer service or visit the establishment with your receipt and explain the issue. They will guide you through their specific refund process

How long it will take food to arrive?

The estimated time for food to arrive can vary depending on the food court's current order volume and your location. On average, it typically takes between 15 to 20 minutes for delivery. However, during peak hours or if the food court is particularly busy, it might take a bit longer.

Does your food court has both veg and non veg?

Yes, our food court offers a diverse menu with both vegetarian and non-vegetarian options. We take pride in catering to a variety of dietary preferences, ensuring there's something delicious for everyone to enjoy. From fresh salads and vegetable dishes to flavorful meats, you'll find a wide range of choices to suit your taste.

What is cost of food delivery?

The cost of food delivery from a food court typically includes the price of the ordered items, a delivery fee, and sometimes a service fee. The total amount can vary based on the food court's pricing, the distance to the delivery location, and any applicable promotions or discounts. It's best to check the food court's menu and delivery policies for specific details.

Who is eligible for pro membership?

Our pro membership is designed for avid food enthusiasts who frequently dine with us and seek exclusive benefits. To be eligible, customers need to have dined at our restaurant at least five times in the past year and subscribe to our newsletter for the latest updates.
